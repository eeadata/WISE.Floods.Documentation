RiskZone#

Description#

RiskZone: Area of Potential Significant Flood Risk

Reporting of spatial elements is no longer mandatory. The QC XC005 requirement is no longer a BLOCKER, and it is now possible to report a Unit of Management containing only the mandatory descriptive table: AreaOfPotentialSignificantFloodRisk_Methodology.

levelOrIntensity is presumed always ‘significant’

Uniqueness: thematicIdIdentifier + thematicIdIdentifierScheme
